The EP's title track, "Typical of Me," sets the tone with its soulful vocals, lush arrangements, and introspective lyrics, drawing listeners into Laufey's intimate storytelling. "Typical of Me" not only showcases Laufey's musical prowess but also hints at the promising future ahead for this talented artist.
